Salsa Chicken

The Perfect College Kid Recipe!

Salsa Chicken only requires three ingredients and a crock pot! This is an adaptation of my Mom's recipe...just easier! This recipe can also be doubled for weekly meal prep or frozen to be eaten later. The possibilities are endless! My roommates and I enjoy this meal at least once a month.

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • 1 Package of Taco Seasoning
  • 1 Jar of Salsa

Recipe

  1. Place chicken breasts, package of taco seasoning, and the jar of salsa in the crock pot. Feel free to add more seasonings (or hot sauce) to desired taste.
  2. Stir to combine the ingredients and place lid on top.
  3. Cook on high for 3 hours or on low for 6 hours.
  4. Shred using 2 forks or hand mixer. If using hand mixer, use low speed to avoid splashing.
  5. Let cook for 15 more minutes on low.
  6. Serve as meat for tacos, burritos, bowls, or whatever else you can think of! Add your favorite toppings and enjoy!

Prep Time: 5 minutes Cook Time: 3-6 hours Yields: 6 meals
